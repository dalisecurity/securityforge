# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and inclusive
- Providing constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility for mistakes
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Sharing knowledge and helping others

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discriminatory language
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Sharing malicious payloads for unauthorized use
- Encouraging illegal activities

## Security and Ethical Standards

Given this project involves security testing:

Acceptable:
- Educational and authorized research
- Responsible disclosure
- Testing with proper authorization

Unacceptable:
- Unauthorized testing
- Malicious use of payloads
- Violating bug bounty rules

## Enforcement

Report issues to: conduct@dalisecurity.com

All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly.
